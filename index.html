Why do I need the Headway app?
On the Headway app, you will find 15-minute summaries of the world’s best nonfiction books. We’ve remastered all titles into short reads with key insights and tips. It will help you read more, learn something new every day, and decide what books you are most interested in reading.
How do I get access to the app?
After you fill in your email address, a mail with a temporary password and download links to App Store and Google Play will be in your inbox. Just install the app, and you will be able to access your account.
How can I cancel my subsctiption?
To cancel the subscription, click the "Unsubscribe" button in your account settings on the website. And don’t forget to cancel 24h before the end date! Otherwise, the payment for the next period will be made automatically.
